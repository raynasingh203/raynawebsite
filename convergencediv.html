Growth of the Indian M&E industry

The Indian M&E industry is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.5 per cent during FY19 to FY24 to reach a size of INR3,070 billion by FY24

The growth of the M&E industry in India is marked by the incorporation of global content trends and formats. The influence of international storytelling techniques, production standards, and visual aesthetics contributes to a convergence of Indian and global cultural elements.
The rise of digital platforms has transformed them into cultural bridges, connecting audiences with content from different cultures.
